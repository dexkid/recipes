Ingredients:
 - 4 slices of bread
 - 2 Tomatoes
 - 1 Cucumber
 - 2 Potatoes
 - Some butter
 - 1 teaspoon salt

Directions:
 1. Boil the potatoes and then peel them.

 2. Slice the vegetables thinly.

 3. Apply butter on 1 face of 2 slices of bread.

 4. On one side of bread, layout the potatoes, cucumber and tomatoes

 5. Sprinkle salt as per taste.

 6. Keep the other slice of bread on top of this.

 7. Now have it directly or toast it for 5 min and then have it.
